Catch App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Catch (the "App"), operated by Catch, LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and shares your personal information. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through compliance with this policy.  This policy applies to all users of the App, regardless of their location.  This policy applies only to information we collect in the App, and in email, text, and other electronic communications sent through or in connection with the App.

If you do not agree with this policy, do not download the App.  By downloading, creating an account, or using the App, you consent to this privacy policy.  This policy may change from time to time (see Changes to This Privacy Policy).  Your continued use of the App after we revise this policy means you accept those changes, so please check the policy periodically for updates.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Information You Provide Directly:
  • Name: When you create an account, we collect your name to identify you within the App and during gameplay.
  • Location Data: The App's core functionality relies on sharing your real-time location with other players during active games of tag. We collect your location using GPS and network-based technologies while the App is in use and with your permission. This allows other players to see your approximate location for gameplay purposes. This data is essential for the App's functionality.
  • Email Address: We collect your email address when you register for an account. This is used for account management, communication regarding the App (e.g., updates, important notices), and password recovery.
  • Information We Collect Automatically:
  • Device Information: We may collect information about any device you use to access the App, including the device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers. This helps us optimize the App for different devices and troubleshoot any technical issues.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, such as the features you use, the games you participate in, and the duration of your sessions. This data helps us understand how users engage with the App and identify areas for improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the App: This includes enabling the core functionality of the App, such as allowing users to initiate and join games of tag and share their real-time location with other participants during gameplay.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your user account, verify your identity, and provide customer support.
  • Communication: To communicate with you regarding your account, provide updates about the App, respond to your inquiries, and send important notices.
  • Improving the App: To analyze how users interact with the App, identify areas for improvement, and develop new features.
  • Personalization (if applicable): To personalize your experience within the App, such as recommending friends or game modes.
  • Security and Fraud Prevention: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, security breaches, and other harmful activity.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  • User Contributions: “User Contributions: You may also provide information for publication or display on public areas of the app (collectively, "User Contributions"). Your User Contributions are Posted and transmitted to others at your own risk. Please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable. Additionally, we cannot control the actions of third parties with whom you may choose to share your User Contributions. Therefore, we cannot and do not guarantee that your User Contributions will not be viewed by unauthorized persons.”

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with the following categories of recipients:

  • Other Users: Your real-time location will be visible to other users participating in the same active game of tag as you. Your name may also be visible to other users within the App for identification purposes.
  • Service Providers: We may engage third-party service providers to assist us with various functions, such as hosting, data analytics, customer support, and email communication. These providers may have access to your personal information to perform these services on our behalf, and they are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal Authorities: We may disclose your information to law enforcement agencies, government bodies, or other third parties if required by law, legal process, or a valid government request.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, sale of assets, or other business transaction, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity or other third parties involved.
  • Aggregated and Anonymized Data: We may share aggregated or anonymized data that does not directly identify you with third parties for research, analytics, or other purposes.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include storing data on safe and secure servers and networks, not sharing any data with unauthorized parties, and encrypting data in transit. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide you with the App's services, maintain your account, and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When you delete your account, we will take reasonable steps to delete your personal information from our active systems, although some information may be retained in our backups for a limited period for security, audit, or legal purposes. Location data collected during gameplay is typically retained only for the duration of the active game session.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age.  If we learn we have collected or received personal information from a child under 13 we will delete that information.  If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.
